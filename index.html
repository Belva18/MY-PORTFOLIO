Who am I?

Academic Background

During my childhood in Cameroon, I attended the following primary schools:

  1. Mulan Bilingual Academic Institute: Located in Kumba, on "Alaska Street"
  2. Mambou International School: In Bafoussam, I attended class 6, where I earned my first school leaving certificate and passed the Common Entrance Examination with distinctions

I came to Ghana after primary school and attended:

  1. Christian Missionary Bilingual School: Located in Accra, at Ogbojo-East Legon
  2. Kwabenya Senior High School: Attended from 2022 to 2024.
    I held the position of health prefect.
    I became a STEAM ambassador after a STEM workshop encouraging girls in STEM.
    I was part of the National Science and Maths Quiz team.

I am currently at Wisconsin International University studying for a degree in Information Technology. In my first semester, I participated in the "CODED WOMEN E-SPORT LEAGUE" coding competition.
